How to Create a YouTube Timestamp Link?

Sharing a long YouTube video can be frustrating if you only want to show a specific moment. Using our YouTube Timestamp Generator, you can create a "deep link" that jumps directly to the seconds you choose.

Method 1: Use this Tool (Easiest)

  1. Copy the URL of the video from your browser or app.
  2. Paste it into the box above.
  3. Enter the exact hour, minute, and second you want the video to start.
  4. Click "Generate Link" and copy the result.

Method 2: Add the Timestamp Manually

If you prefer to do it yourself, you can modify the URL by adding a query parameter to the end of the link:

  • For standard links: Add &t=1m30s to the end (for 1 minute 30 seconds).
  • For short links (youtu.be): Add ?t=1m30s to the end.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why isn't my timestamp working?

The most common reason is using the wrong separator. Standard YouTube URLs (watch?v=...) require an & (ampersand), while shortened URLs (youtu.be/...) require a ? (question mark) before the "t". Our tool handles this automatically.

Can I timestamp YouTube Shorts?

Yes! However, the "Shorts" player doesn't always support time jumps perfectly. We recommend converting the "shorts" URL to a standard "watch" URL, which our tool does for you automatically.

Does this work on Mobile?

Yes. The generated links work on both desktop browsers and the YouTube mobile app (iOS and Android).

Is my data safe? Do you store my links?

Absolutely safe! This tool runs entirely in your browser (client-side). We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to our servers. Your YouTube links and timestamps are processed locally on your device and never leave your computer. Feel free to use this tool with complete confidence.
